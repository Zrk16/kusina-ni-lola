37 Years of Lutong-Bahay
Ang Aming Kwento
"Kapag kumain ka sa aming kusina, parang nasa bahay ka ng iyong lola."

In 1987, Lola Nena started cooking for her neighbors in Tungkong Mangga with nothing but a wood-fire stove and a heart full of love. What began as feeding a few families grew into something the whole barangay came to depend on.

Today, her daughter Ana and grandson Kuya Marco carry on that tradition — the same recipes, the same warmth, the same table open for everyone.
