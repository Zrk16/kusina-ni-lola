Why People Come Back

Three things that
haven't changed.

01

Fresh Every Morning

We buy from the palengke before sunrise. Every pot is cooked same-day. Nothing gets reheated, nothing gets frozen — if it's not fresh, it doesn't go on the plate. That rule has held since 1987.

02

Recipes by Heart

Lola Nena's sinigang, adobo, and kare-kare have never been written down. They live in memory, adjusted by taste and instinct — the way real lutong-bahay has always worked. No packet mixes, ever.

03

A Table for Everyone

Priced so the whole barangay can eat well. Open on holidays. We've never turned away a hungry neighbor. That's not a policy — it's just who Lola Nena is.

In 1987, Lola Nena set up a clay pot and wood-fire stove in front of her home and cooked sinigang for her neighbors. Word spread fast. Three generations later, the carinderia is still there — same street, same recipes, same care.
